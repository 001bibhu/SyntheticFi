# Feature requests

Suggest new capabilities, integrations, or improvements to SyntheticFi. Product ideas belong here — use [Create a ticket](/support/create-ticket) for bugs and account issues.

## What happens next

1. **Triage** — Product team reviews within 5 business days
2. **Clarification** — We may email follow-up questions
3. **Prioritization** — Scored by impact, alignment, and effort
4. **Notification** — Email when status changes

## Good requests include

- The **problem** you're solving (not just a solution)
- Who benefits — advisors, investors, partners
- How often the need comes up
- Any workaround you use today

## Integration requests

For new custodians or brokerages, include institution name, approximate AUM affected, and willingness to join a beta program.

Submissions appear in the [analytics dashboard](/admin/analytics) alongside feedback trends.
